This dynamic computing module continuously monitors internal cellular temperatures and dynamically throttles the current to prevent catastrophic thermal runaway. By actively regulating the thermal envelope, the A110D-P11 significantly extends the operational lifespan of the internal lithium-ion cells while rigorously safeguarding the receiving devices against voltage spikes and localized overheating.
